# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Homeowners and small-business owners who check in on their surveillance cameras occasionally, not full-time security staff monitoring feeds all day. Sessions are brief and infrequent: someone opens the app to confirm things are fine, not to stare at a wall of feeds for hours. The UI should stay low-friction and quick to scan rather than dense, power-user tooling.

## Product Purpose

A surveillance camera manager: login gates access, and the home screen exists to answer one question fast — "is everything okay right now?" Success looks like a user glancing at the app and immediately trusting the status they see, with zero ambiguity about whether something needs their attention.

## Brand Personality

Technical and precise — engineer-grade, no-nonsense, quietly confident. Not soft or playful, but also not alarmist or clinical-cold: it should read as a serious, exacting tool that still feels calm and trustworthy to someone checking in briefly, not intimidating or built only for full-time operators.

## Anti-references

Not a generic SaaS dashboard: no cream/white near-default backgrounds, no gradient hero-metric tiles, no identical icon+heading+text card grids. Should feel distinct and purpose-built, not templated.

## Design Principles

- **Precision over prettiness** — technical exactness (tight alignment, deliberate type, no decorative filler) over generic polish.
- **At-a-glance status** — the home screen's first job is answering "is everything fine?" instantly; status must never require reading or interpretation.
- **Confidence without alarm** — calm, technical tone even in a security-flavored product; reserve visual alarm (strong reds, aggressive motion) for genuine alerts, not default chrome.
- **Distinct from SaaS-cream convention** — a technical, dark-mode-native identity, not a templated dashboard look.
- **Low-friction for occasional use** — optimize for a quick, infrequent check-in, not continuous power-user monitoring.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA contrast (body text ≥4.5:1, large text ≥3:1). Full keyboard navigation. Reduced-motion alternative for every animation.
